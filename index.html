Why Choose Our Vendor Management System? & Who We Are

Elite Manpower and Training Academy (EMTA), founded in 2020 by visionaries Niki Bose and Abhijeet Raghuwanshi, is a trailblazer in BFSI training and job placement. With a mission to transform conventional placement processes, EMTA is devoted to closing the knowledge gap between academia and business needs. The academy has grown over time into a full facility that provides training, career progression programs, and industry-aligned direct job placement services.
